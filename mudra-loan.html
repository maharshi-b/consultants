Mudra Loans

‘Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana’ (PMMY) was launched to ‘fund the unfunded’ by bringing such enterprises to the formal financial system, and extending affordable credit to them. Any micro or small business owner can apply for an SBI Mudra loan. The owners can be proprietors, partners or directors in the business. There is no subsidy provided under Mudra loan scheme.
Variants of the MUDRA loans :
  1. Shishu (upto Rs. 50,000), Margin : Nil
  2. Kishore (above Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 5 lakh), Margin : 10%
  3. Tarun (above Rs. 5 lakh to Rs. 10 lakh), Margin : 10%
Rate of Interest :

The rate of interest applicable to the MSME units is in the range of MCLR to MCLR +2.25% (subject to change)


Repayment Period :

Term Loan 3 Yrs to 5 Years, Working Capital on Demand, Moratorium Period : 6 Months.


Sanction /Followup :

Sanctioned by the Bank’s / Followed-up by Govt. of India.


Time Taken by Consultant :

2 days After completion of documents by Borrower.


Service Offered :
  • Preparing the Project Report.
  • Helping in lodging on Bank/ Udami Mitra Portal.
  • Replying the queries of bank
